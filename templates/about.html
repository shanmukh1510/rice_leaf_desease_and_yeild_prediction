About SmartRice

AI-powered agriculture intelligence platform

Our Mission

SmartRice is an AI-powered precision agriculture platform designed to empower rice farmers with the power of Deep Learning and Machine Learning. We help farmers detect leaf diseases early and predict crop yields accurately — reducing losses and maximizing productivity.

Built with TensorFlow/Keras for image classification and Scikit-Learn for yield regression, SmartRice brings enterprise-level AI to every farmer's pocket.
